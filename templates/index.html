YOU MUST FIRST REGISTER AND LOGIN IF YOU WANT TO SAVE YOUR SCORE!

The Player can be moved with the Arrow Keys or with the WASD keys.

The aim of the game is avoid the Crabs chasing the Player while also trying to kite the Crabs so that they end up colliding with one of the Obstacles as the Crabs are very brittle and die easily. The Player gains score with each destroyed Crab .

The Player will die if they make any contact with a Crab or if the player makes any contact with the Water . Crabs can freely traverse the Water unlike the player.

Periodically Bombs will spawn and if the Player makes contact with them, all Crabs currently on screen will be removed and the players score will incremented accordingly.

Upon achieving a score of 50 the game will end as the Player has one. Given the existence of Bombs , it is possible to end the game with a score of above 50 if the Player uses a Bomb while at 49 and there are more than 1 Crabs present at that time.

Logged in users can save their score to the leaderboard.
